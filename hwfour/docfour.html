  1. Contrast
  • Example: Wordle Website
    • Wordle
    Reason 1
    The use of white letters and grid against a black background is high contrast, creating a distinct visualization.
    Reason 2
    The spare use of green and yellow set certain letters apart indicating something special with them in the game.
    Reason 3
    They use a combination of a serif font for the heading and sans serif for text.
